This dashboard provides a 360-degree view of Blinkit's retail operations, focusing on sales trends, inventory distribution, and outlet efficiency.

1. High-Level Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
At a glance, the dashboard captures the core health of the business:

Total Sales: $1.20M, indicating a strong market presence.

Average Sales: $141 per transaction.

Number of Items: 8,523 unique items tracked across inventory.

Average Rating: 3.9 stars, showing consistent customer satisfaction.

2. Temporal Trends (Outlet Establishment)
The area chart shows the growth trajectory of outlets from 2010 to 2022.

There is a significant peak in 2018 ($205K), followed by a stabilization around the $130K mark in recent years, suggesting a mature market phase.

3. Product & Inventory Insights
Fat Content Analysis: Regular fat products dominate sales ($776.32K) compared to Low Fat ($425.36K). This is further broken down by outlet tiers, showing Tier 3 locations are the biggest contributors to both categories.

Item Type Breakdown: Fruits, Vegetables, and Snack Foods are the top revenue generators ($0.18M each), highlighting the high demand for perishables and quick-consumption goods.

4. Outlet & Geography Segmentation
Outlet Size: Medium-sized outlets are the top performers ($507.90K), followed by Small and High-capacity stores.

Outlet Location: Tier 3 cities lead in sales volume ($472.13K), followed by Tier 2 and Tier 1. This suggests that Blinkit has successfully penetrated deeper into suburban or developing urban areas.

Outlet Type Performance: Supermarket Type 1 is the powerhouse of the operation, managing the highest volume of items (5,577) while maintaining competitive average sales and ratings.

5. Advanced Metrics
The "Outlet Type" table provides a deep dive into efficiency, showing that while Grocery Stores have lower item counts, they maintain a high Item Visibility (0.10), likely due to a more curated product selection.
