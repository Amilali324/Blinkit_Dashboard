3. Product & Inventory Insights

Outlet Location: Tier 3 cities lead in sales volume ($472.13K), followed by Tier 2 and Tier 1. This suggests that Blinkit has successfully penetrated deeper into suburban or developing urban areas.

Outlet Type Performance: Supermarket Type 1 is the powerhouse of the operation, managing the highest volume of items (5,577) while maintaining competitive average sales and ratings.
